# Open Graph Image - Required for Social Sharing

## What You Need

Create an image named `og-image.jpg` and place it in the `/public` folder.

## Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 1200px × 630px (Facebook/LinkedIn recommended)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 5MB (ideally under 1MB)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1.91:1

## Content Suggestions

The image should include:
1. **Elite Detailing** logo/brand name
2. Luxury vehicle or jet image (high quality)
3. Tagline: "Luxury Auto & Private Jet Detailing"
4. Service areas: "Serving New England"
5. Brand colors: Gold (#D4AF37) and Black (#0A0A0A)

## Tools to Create

- **Canva** (easiest): https://www.canva.com/create/open-graph/
- **Figma** (design tool): Use template for 1200×630
- **Photoshop**: Create custom design

## Why This Matters

When people share your website on:
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- Twitter
- WhatsApp
- iMessage

This image will appear as a preview card, making your link look professional and clickable.
